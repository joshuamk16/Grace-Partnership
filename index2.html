Grace Partnership, Inc.

Leo D. Cho, AIA, LEED, is the founding Principal of Grace Partnership Inc. of Los Angeles, which has developed a strong reputation for the design of a wide range of major shopping centers, residences, retails, plants, restaurants, and religious centers. He graduated from the University of Hawaii, Manoa in 1989 with background of mechanical engineering from University of California, Berkeley. Currently, he heads the design and the research branch of Grace Partnership Inc.

Stephen Kim is the founding Partner of Grace Partnership Inc. of Los Angeles. He graduated from the Southern California Institute of Architecture in 1991 with background of business management from University of California, Los Angeles. As a licensed general contractor in State of California, he heads the design-build branch of Grace Partnership Inc. with extensive experience in construction of residential, retail, and restaurant.

Overview

Grace Partnership was founded in synergy when Leo Cho and Stephen Kim merged their thriving practices together in 1998. Since the inception, the two partners have been actively leading project teams in a practice founded on a structure of participation, efficiency and empowerment. Grace Partnership is a full-service architectural firm with a capacity to materialize projects of all sizes, from planning and conceptualization to contract administration and construction management.

Vision

Integrity and competency are the driving philosophy of our practice. We believe that our clients' requirements and wishes should be the foremost important starting point of projects. We are committed to bring their visions and dreams in to reality, not only by functionally efficient design, but also by graceful design that enrich the lives of people who use them. Our clients and team of consultants are collaborative partners in every journey to discover the best solution. We look forward to decades more working closely with you to build our and your business together, by designing places that capture physical, cultural and spiritual significance over time.
